Virginia’s climate presents real and ongoing challenges for commercial roofs. Hot, humid summers put constant stress on roofing membranes, causing expansion, contraction, and accelerated wear. Cold winters bring ice, snow, and freeze-thaw cycles that can compromise seams, flashings, and drainage systems. Severe thunderstorms and heavy rainfall test the waterproofing performance of every commercial roof. Virginia Roof Pro understands these conditions and installs systems specifically suited to perform reliably through every season that Lynchburg, VA has to offer.

Why quality matters in commercial roofing: A well-installed commercial roof protects not just your building, but your inventory, equipment, employees, and reputation. Choosing the best commercial roofing contractor in Lynchburg, VA means investing in craftsmanship that pays for itself over time through reduced maintenance costs and maximum system longevity.

Our commercial roofing services cover a comprehensive range of low-slope and flat roof systems. We install TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin) roofing, a popular single-ply membrane known for its energy efficiency, reflectivity, and resistance to UV exposure and weathering. We also specialize in EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) roofing, a durable rubber membrane with an outstanding track record for low-maintenance performance. For buildings requiring a more traditional approach, our modified bitumen systems offer excellent waterproofing performance with multiple layers of reinforced asphalt-based materials. Every system we recommend is matched to the specific demands of your building and budget.

Virginia Roof Pro is fully licensed and insured, and every commercial roofing project we undertake is backed by both manufacturer warranties on materials and our own workmanship guarantee.
