What Sets Our Roof Inspection Apart from the Competition

Not all roof inspections are created equal. A cursory walk-around inspection might catch obvious damage, but it will miss the subtle early-warning signs that lead to costly repairs down the road. Virginia Roof Pro’s inspection protocol was developed specifically with Virginia’s climate in mind. Our 25-point process covers every critical component of your roofing system, inside and out:

  • Shingle and surface condition — We examine granule loss, cracking, curling, and blistering that signal age-related deterioration.
  • Flashing integrity — Chimney, skylight, and valley flashing failures are among the most common sources of leaks in Lynchburg homes.
  • Deck and structural assessment — We probe the decking for soft spots or sagging that indicate underlying rot or structural compromise.
  • Ventilation review — Poor attic ventilation accelerates shingle aging and leads to ice dam formation during Lynchburg’s cold snaps.
  • Gutter and drainage evaluation — Clogged or damaged gutters redirect water directly into your fascia and foundation.
  • Moss, algae, and biological growth — Common in shaded areas near the Blackwater Creek Trail and other wooded neighborhoods.
  • Storm and hail damage documentation — Critical for insurance claims after severe weather events.

When Should You Schedule an Inspection?

We recommend Lynchburg homeowners schedule professional roof inspections at least once a year. The ideal times are spring — to assess winter damage — and fall, before temperatures drop and minor issues become major problems under ice and snow load. Additional inspections are strongly advised after any significant weather event, including the severe thunderstorms that roll in from the Blue Ridge Mountains during summer and the occasional ice storm that coats the city in January and February. If you are buying or selling a home near Percival’s Island, Downtown Lynchburg, or anywhere in the greater metro area, a pre-transaction roof inspection is an invaluable investment that protects both parties.
