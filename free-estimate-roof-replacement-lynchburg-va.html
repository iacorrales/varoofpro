Step 1: Schedule Your Free On-Site Inspection

The first step is simple: contact us. A member of our team will schedule a time that works for you — evenings and Saturdays are available for busy Lynchburg families. When our roofing specialist arrives, they’ll introduce themselves, discuss any concerns you’ve already noticed, and get to work on the inspection.

This isn’t a quick five-minute walkthrough. Our specialists take the time to thoroughly examine every aspect of your roof system, including the shingles, flashing around chimneys and vents, the ridge cap, gutters, soffit, and fascia. We look for signs of age, storm damage, moisture intrusion, and structural issues that might affect the scope of your replacement.

Step 2: Thorough Roof Assessment

Lynchburg’s location at the foothills of the Blue Ridge Mountains means local homes experience a wide range of weather conditions throughout the year. Summers bring intense heat and humidity, winters can include ice and snow, and spring and fall often arrive with heavy rainfall and windstorms that can accelerate roof deterioration. Our specialists understand these regional conditions and assess your roof accordingly.

We’ll measure the square footage, evaluate the pitch and complexity of your roof, inspect the decking for any signs of rot or damage, and check attic ventilation — a critical factor that affects how long your new roof will last. Proper attic ventilation is especially important in Lynchburg, where summer attic temperatures can exceed 150 degrees Fahrenheit, causing premature shingle degradation if airflow is inadequate.

Step 3: Material Selection and Recommendations

Once the inspection is complete, we’ll sit down with you — either on-site or at a time of your choosing — and walk through your material options. Most Lynchburg homeowners opt for architectural asphalt shingles because of their balance of affordability, durability, and curb appeal. However, we also offer:

  • Metal roofing – Exceptional longevity, often 40–70 years, and ideal for handling the heavier snow loads that occasionally affect higher-elevation areas near Percival’s Island and the western reaches of the city.
  • Impact-resistant shingles – Rated for hail and high winds, providing an extra layer of protection and potentially reducing your homeowner’s insurance premiums.
  • Designer and premium shingles – For homeowners in Lynchburg’s historic districts who want superior aesthetics that complement the character of their neighborhoods near Downtown Lynchburg.

Step 4: Receive Your Detailed Written Estimate

At the conclusion of our assessment, we provide a fully itemized written estimate. No vague line items, no surprise add-ons at the end of the job. Your estimate will clearly state the scope of work, all materials to be used (with specific product names and warranties), labor costs, estimated timeline, and payment options. We believe complete transparency is the foundation of a trustworthy contractor-homeowner relationship.

Many Lynchburg homeowners tell us that getting our written estimate was the first time they truly understood what a roof replacement involves. We’re happy to answer every question you have — about materials, warranties, the installation process, or anything else on your mind.
