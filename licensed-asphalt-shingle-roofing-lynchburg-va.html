Virginia’s Climate Demands Quality Asphalt Shingle Roofing

Virginia’s climate presents real challenges for residential roofs. Lynchburg homeowners experience hot, humid summers that can accelerate shingle aging, cold winters with ice and snow that place stress on roofing materials, and seasonal severe thunderstorms with heavy rain and strong winds. Freeze-thaw cycles throughout the colder months can cause existing damage to worsen rapidly. A properly installed asphalt shingle roof, completed by licensed professionals using quality materials, is your first line of defense against these conditions year after year.

What Is Asphalt Shingle Roofing?

Asphalt shingles are the most popular residential roofing material in the United States, and for good reason. They offer a strong combination of affordability, durability, and aesthetic versatility. Available in three-tab, architectural (dimensional), and premium impact-resistant grades, asphalt shingles can complement virtually any home style while providing dependable weather protection. A quality asphalt shingle roof, properly installed on a well-ventilated and prepared deck, can provide 20–30 years or more of reliable service.
