Common Storm Damage We Repair in Lynchburg

Virginia’s Central Piedmont region subjects roofs to a wide range of severe weather threats throughout the year. Our experienced roofers regularly address the following types of storm-related damage for Lynchburg homeowners:

  • Hail damage: Hail impacts create bruising and granule loss on asphalt shingles, weakening their protective barrier and accelerating aging.
  • Wind damage: High winds can lift, crack, or completely remove shingles, exposing the roof deck to moisture intrusion.
  • Fallen tree & branch damage: Trees along the James River corridor and the wooded neighborhoods near Blackwater Creek Trail can fall onto roofs during major storm events.
  • Flashing damage: Storm winds can loosen or separate flashing around chimneys, skylights, and vents — creating hidden leak pathways.
  • Soffit & fascia damage: Wind-driven rain and debris frequently damage soffits and fascia boards, which protect the roof’s edge and attic ventilation.
  • Ice dam formation: Cold snaps following storms can create ice dams that force water beneath shingles, causing interior ceiling leaks.
  • Gutter damage: Heavy debris and water volume from storms can bend, pull, or detach gutters from the fascia, compromising roof drainage.
