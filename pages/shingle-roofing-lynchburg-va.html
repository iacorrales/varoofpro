How long do shingle roofs last in Lynchburg, VA's climate?

In Lynchburg's central Virginia climate, quality architectural asphalt shingles typically last 25 to 30 years. The region's mix of hot, humid summers, occasional ice storms, and proximity to the Blue Ridge Mountains means roofs face significant temperature swings and moisture exposure. Proper installation and periodic maintenance can extend the lifespan considerably. Older 3-tab shingles common on homes built before 2000 typically last 15 to 20 years and may already be due for replacement.
